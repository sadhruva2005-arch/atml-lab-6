T1 & T2 En→Hi · Bidirectional Attention Seq2Seq

Bidirectional LSTM encoder + Bahdanau attention decoder. Vocabulary frequency pruning (min_freq=2) with short-sentence filtering for cleaner data. Cosine annealing LR schedule over 20 epochs.

T3 En→Es · MarianMT (Helsinki-NLP)

Transformer encoder-decoder pre-trained on the OPUS dataset. Fine-tunable via HuggingFace Seq2SeqTrainer on opus_books.

T4 Summarization · BART-large-CNN

Denoising autoencoder with Transformer encoder-decoder. Abstractive summarization evaluated by compression ratio. Supports configurable min/max output lengths.
